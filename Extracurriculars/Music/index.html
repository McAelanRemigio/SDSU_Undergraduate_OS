Overview

Music has been part of my life for as long as I can remember. Growing up in a Filipino household, singing was everywhere—family gatherings, celebrations, karaoke nights, and community events.


At San Diego State University, music became one of the ways I stayed connected to that part of myself. Unlike my academic and leadership work, this was something I pursued purely out of love for performance and expression.


A consistent thread across nearly all of my solo performances has been romance music. Songs about love, connection, and relationships became my primary storytelling medium, allowing me to communicate emotion in a direct and human way.

What I Did

Campus Performances

Performed at SDSU events including Aztec Night Market, APSA Formal, Battle of the Bands, and various cultural showcases. Audience sizes ranged from ~100 to 500+ attendees.


Solo & Duet Work

Focused primarily on solo and duet performances featuring jazz standards, crooner-style music, swing influences, and romantic ballads.


Competitive Performance

Selected as a Top 4 competing act in a campus talent competition and placed Runner-Up performing before a live audience of 500+ attendees.
