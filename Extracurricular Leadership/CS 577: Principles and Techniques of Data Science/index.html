Overview

After founding the Data Science Initiative at SDSU and transitioning leadership to its next team, I moved into CS 577 as an Instructional Student Assistant.


CS 577 is an upper-division and graduate-level course focused on modern data science principles, workflows, and applied techniques. In this role, I support curriculum development and help structure learning materials that guide students through both conceptual understanding and technical implementation.

What I’m Building

Curriculum & Course Materials

  • Co-develop instructional materials for CS 577
  • Help organize course topics around core data science workflows
  • Support assignment design and learning resource development

Applied Data Science Learning

  • Help students connect theory to practical implementation
  • Support reproducible, project-based learning approaches
  • Contribute to assignments emphasizing real data workflows

Student Instructional Support

  • Assist students across undergraduate and graduate levels
  • Clarify technical and conceptual misunderstandings
  • Support learning across diverse technical backgrounds

Outcomes

  • Contributed to development and refinement of CS 577 materials
  • Improved assignment clarity and instructional structure
  • Supported students across varying levels of technical experience
  • Identified recurring learning gaps to improve course design
  • Strengthened emphasis on reproducible, applied data science workflows

Leadership Tradeoffs

Unlike founding a student organization, this role focuses on improving an existing academic system where clarity and structure matter more than visibility or creation.


  • Clarity over complexity — simplifying without losing accuracy
  • Structure over improvisation — designing step-by-step learning paths
  • Support over spotlight — impact happens through student outcomes, not visibility

What I’m Learning

  • How to design structured learning systems in data science
  • Curriculum sequencing and scaffolding principles
  • How students with different backgrounds learn technical material
  • Translating complex ideas into actionable explanations
  • Iterative improvement of educational systems
  • How teaching and data science both rely on system design thinking

How This Connects

CS 577 extends the same theme that began with the Data Science Initiative: building systems that help people understand and use data effectively.


One created access. The other refines understanding.
