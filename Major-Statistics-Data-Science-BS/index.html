Overview

The Statistics & Data Science major integrates statistical theory, mathematics, and computational methods to develop analytical systems capable of transforming data into evidence-based decisions.


Coursework spans probability, statistical inference, programming, database systems, machine learning, and applied data science, emphasizing both theoretical understanding and practical implementation.

Lower Division

Foundational coursework in mathematics, programming, and introductory data science concepts that establish the prerequisites for upper division study and research.
