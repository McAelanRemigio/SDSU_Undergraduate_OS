# About This Archive

This repository is a structured record of my undergraduate experience at San Diego State University.

It is not a portfolio of isolated achievements.

It is a system of experiences across multiple domains:
- Academic learning (Data Science)
- Organizational leadership
- Competitive and creative performance
- Reflective writing

---

## How I Approach Work

Across all domains, I tend to operate in systems.

Whether it is:
- analyzing datasets
- building student organizations
- competing in esports
- performing music
- or writing reflective essays

I am drawn to environments where I can understand structure, improve performance, and communicate insights clearly.

---

## What This Represents

This archive reflects how I grew across four dimensions:

- Learning systems (academics)
- Building systems (leadership)
- Competing within systems (performance)
- Reflecting on systems (writing)

---

## Why It Exists

Most undergraduate experiences are remembered in fragments.

This is my attempt to structure those fragments into something coherent.
